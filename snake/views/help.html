How To Play

You control a snake. Your objective is to find delicious red apples and eat them. For every apple, the snake will grow larger. You gain 1 point for each apple.

For every 10 points you earn, you level up. Keep earning points and you will be rewarded for your hard work with achievements. Or simply try to beat your high score.

You must avoid obstacles such as the four walls or any part of the snake itself. This gets harder as the game advances. The snake will take up more space on the board, leaving less space to maneuver the snake.

Controls

  • Left Arrow: Move the snake to the left

  • Right Arrow: Move the snake to the right

  • Up Arrow: Move the snake up

  • Down Arrow: Move the snake down
