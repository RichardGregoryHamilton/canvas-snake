Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: What is this game?

Answer: This is a snake game. You control a snake and maneuver around the game board, searching for apples.

Question 2: How do you earn coins?

Answer: Coins are a reward for your hard work. You earn a certain number of coins after every game. For every five apples the snake eats, you gain 1 coin. You can use these coins to purchase items in the shop.

Question 3: Do you have to create an account?

Answer: No. Best of all, this game can be played without signing up.

Question 4: Do you have to pay to play this game?

Answer: This game can be played for free. However, you can purchase coins and items from the store.
